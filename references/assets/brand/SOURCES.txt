Provenance (fetched 2026-06-11):

logo-pack/            github.com/nimiq/designs (master) — official Nimiq logo pack.
                      All RGB SVGs, flattened from logo/RGB/<color>/svg/.
                      Skipped: CMYK EPS+JPEG print files (~600KB each) and RGB PNG
                      rasters (redundant with SVGs). _extended/ = legacy product
                      logos (keyguard, miner, safe, shop).
nimiq-logo.min.svg    repo root of nimiq/designs (inside logo-pack/).

identicons/           github.com/nimiq/identicons (master).
                      src-svg/ = the 84 named identicon part sources
                      (bottom/face/side/top). identicons.min.svg = compiled
                      symbol sprite (same content the @nimiq/iqons CDN bundle ships).

awesome-apps/         github.com/nimiq/awesome src/data/assets — app icons/cards
                      that nimiq.com hotlinks via raw.githubusercontent.com on its
                      apps section (nim-world.svg, nimiq-hexagon.svg, etc).
awesome-exchanges/    same repo, assets/exchanges — exchange logos.
awesome-mini-apps/    same repo, assets/mini-apps.
awesome-data-nimiq-apps.json  same repo, src/data/dist/nimiq-apps.json —
                      machine-readable app index mapping names -> logo URLs.

nimiq-com-meta/       og.png = https://www.nimiq.com/__og-image__/static/og.png
                      (1200x600). favicon.ico = https://www.nimiq.com/favicon.ico
                      (48/32/16 multi-size). No other favicon/manifest conventions
                      exist on nimiq.com (all 404). /brand /press /media-kit also 404.
